About AWSUG Aotearoa


AWSUG Aotearoa (AWS User Group Aotearoa) is New Zealand's premier community for AWS enthusiasts, professionals, and learners.


Our Mission

We bring together cloud professionals, developers, architects, and enthusiasts to share knowledge, learn from each other, and grow the AWS community across Aotearoa New Zealand.


What We Do

  • Host regular meetups and events across New Zealand
  • Provide a platform for knowledge sharing and networking
  • Connect members with AWS experts and community leaders
  • Support learning and professional development
  • Foster collaboration and innovation in cloud technologies

Get Involved

Whether you're just starting your cloud journey or you're a seasoned AWS professional, there's a place for you in our community. Join our Discord server, attend a meetup, or even become a presenter!


Everyone is welcome - from lurkers to active contributors. We're all in this together.
