Society Initiatives

The AWS User Group Aotearoa is committed to fostering a vibrant and inclusive cloud computing community across New Zealand. Our initiatives are designed to support learning, collaboration, and professional growth for all members.

Current Initiatives

Community Engagement

We organize regular meetups, workshops, and networking events across Auckland, Wellington, and Christchurch to bring together AWS enthusiasts, professionals, and learners.

Knowledge Sharing

Through our events and online platforms, we facilitate the exchange of ideas, best practices, and real-world experiences with AWS technologies.

Professional Development

We support members in their AWS certification journey and career advancement through study groups, mentorship opportunities, and technical presentations.

AWS Community Day

Our flagship annual event brings together the broader Oceania AWS community for a full day of learning, networking, and celebrating cloud innovation.

If you have ideas for new initiatives or would like to get involved, please reach out to our committee members or join us at one of our upcoming events.
