ABOUT AWSUG AOTEAROA

AWSUG Aotearoa (AWS User Group Aotearoa Incorporated Society) is New Zealand's premier community for AWS enthusiasts, professionals, and learners.

OUR MISSION

We bring together cloud professionals, developers, architects, and enthusiasts to share knowledge, learn from each other, and grow the AWS community across Aotearoa New Zealand.

WHAT WE DO

  • Host regular meetups and events across New Zealand
  • Provide a platform for knowledge sharing and networking
  • Connect members with AWS experts and community leaders
  • Support learning and professional development
  • Foster collaboration and innovation in cloud technologies

GET INVOLVED

Whether you're just starting your cloud journey or you're a seasoned AWS professional, there's a place for you in our community. Join our Discord server, join and attend in person meetups, or even become a presenter!

Everyone is welcome. We're all in this together.

HOW ARE WE ORGANISED?

  • Run by a committee made of community members, AWS Partners, and AWS customers
  • Registered as charity organisation under the "Advancement of Education" criterion
  • Bi-weekly meetings for running the meetups and managing the organisation.
