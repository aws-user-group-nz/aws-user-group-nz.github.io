OUR REGULATIONS

These regulations are made by the Committee under sections 8.1 and 25.1 of our constitution.

MEMBERSHIP

  • Anyone can become a member of the society by agreeing to comply with the obligations in section 15 of our constitution and applying to the executive committee.
  • Every member of the society is required to be active in at least one of the societies' committees.
  • The executive committee may decline applications if it so chooses.

COMMITTEES

  • The executive committee (the president, vp, secretary, treasurer, and 1 representative from each of the other committees) - Meetup subcommittee
  • Special events subcommittee (ie. annual conference)

CAREER PROGRESSION SUBCOMMITTEE

Creation of Regulations

  • Regulations are created by the agreement of the executive committee.
  • Proposals for new regulations are to be formatted as github pull requests.
